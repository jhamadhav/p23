ABOUT SRC

Students’ Representative Council Students’ representative council is the official student body of the college, which forms the bridge between the students, management, staff and parents. It was estabilished in the year 1992 and consists of representatives from all the branches. It plays an important role in the overall development of a student, and the institution. SRC provides a platform for the students to adapt new skills, enhance their ability to plan, organize and execute group activities. students also get an opportunity to showcase their talents, participate in co-curricular and extra curricular activities through the domain of SRC. Various clubs have been estabilished, such as dance club, drama club, readers’ reverie literary club, music club, photography club, technical club, art club etc. throughout the year, the stydents’ representative council organizes and manages several social and academic activities and fests, which enable a student to participate, absorb and amend the different skills and talents. Some of the events organized by SRC are- gyanodaya, an induction program for the first year students, aaruni- the fresher’s event for first years, pratishruti- the biggest cultural festival across central india and many more. These help in developing communication skills, organizational skills, leadership skills and many more soft skills which are deemed necessary in the current time. Apart from these, there are several departmental organizations corresponding to the various departments, which cater to the department specific needs and skills, in association with SRC.

ABOUT RCOEM

Thirty years ago, the management of the college set out to create a workforce that would aid in the development of the country. The Shri Ramdeobaba College of Engineering and Management was founded as a result of the achievement of this objective and a commitment to upholding the best standards in engineering and management education (RCOEM). Established in 1984 institution Shri Ramdeobaba college of engineering and management. "Eminent Engineering Education" is the college's motto.
