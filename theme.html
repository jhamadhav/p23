WHEN?

With time this generation is resorting to ways of living, which tends to deteriorate the originality of their nature. The only thing that one can allude to themselves is lost in the way. The track leading to a destination is changed altering and derailing the original journey.

WHY?

We look into the mirror and all we see is a corporate dud who gave up to the rat race, surfing the past all you see is-us there smiling-and all it does is haunt you. Why sabotage your true self just to earn something that won't even last with you?

Why vandalize your uniqueness, your creativity, something that you dreamt of as a child? Go read that book that's been napping there on your shelf, go hug your people the ones that need some love, go paint that painting that is thirsty, quenching for colours, go live that life that you've always dreamt to live

WILL

The theme focuses on discovering your true self, the self that you imagined yourself to be in the childhood looking out of the window, staring at the sky laying on wet grass hoping one day, to be something and someone who makes people's life better. Who loves people, and importantly loves himself. At last mere yaar, Dil khush jaha, teri manzil he vaha!
