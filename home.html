Pratishruti, the largest cultural festival in central India, is an extravagant celebration of culture for colleges in central India. During this time, both intercollegiate and intercollegiate events are held. The festival consists primarily of cultural events as well as educational and motivational sessions, during which guest speakers from a variety of genres will discuss important self-development issues. College teams from all over central India have participated in pratishruti competitions. Dramatics, literary arts, speaking arts, visual arts, film & media, music, dance, and esports are all included in competitions

On-spot registrations may or may not be allowed depending on the number of registered participants. The registration fee might also differ afterwards. We suggest that you register as soon as the registrations open.
